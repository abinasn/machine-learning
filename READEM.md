# machine-learning

> A JavaScript developer's honest journey into Data Science and Machine Learning.

---

## Who am I?

I'm Abinas — a full-stack JavaScript developer who builds things for the web.

For years, I've worked with frontend and backend systems, APIs, databases, and everything in between. I know how to make things work. But somewhere along the way, I started wondering about a different kind of problem — not *how to build* things, but *how to make things learn*.

That curiosity brought me here.

---

## What is this repo?

This is my learning journal.

Not a collection of polished projects. Not a showcase of things I already know.
This is the **raw, honest, day-by-day record** of me learning Data Science and Machine Learning from absolute scratch — while working full time as a developer.

Every note I write here is in my own words.
Every notebook I commit here is code I typed myself.
Every concept in here is something I genuinely struggled to understand — and then finally did.

---

## The commitment

On **March 24, 2026**, I made a decision.

**30 minutes every single day. No exceptions. No skipping. Until December 31, 2026.**

Hard start. Hard stop. Consistent over intense.

I am not trying to learn everything at once. I am trying to build something that lasts — a real understanding of how machine learning works, from the ground up.

## Why am I doing this publicly?

Accountability.

When nobody is watching, it's easy to skip a day. Easy to say "I'll catch up tomorrow."
Making this public means the streak is real. The progress is real. The gaps are real too.

If you're reading this and you're on a similar journey — I hope this repo reminds you that it's okay to start slow. It's okay to not understand everything immediately. What matters is that you show up every day.
